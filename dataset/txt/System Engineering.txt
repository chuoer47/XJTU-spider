课程名称：System Engineering
主讲教师：Feng Wu
所在学院：The School of Management
课程团队：Yingluo Wang、Shulin Liu、Gang Li
上线平台：学堂在线

As the platform course of management, the first-class discipline of management science and engineering, and the core course of industrial engineering, systems engineering is an important methodology for management science and engineering, and modern industrial engineering. As systemic problems are becoming increasingly universal, this course plays an important and practical role in helping students of management and other related disciplines master systems engineering methodology and system analysis methods.
