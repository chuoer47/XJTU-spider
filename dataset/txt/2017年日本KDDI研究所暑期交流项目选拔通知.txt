标题: 2017年日本KDDI研究所暑期交流项目选拔通知
发布日期: 2017-04-14
浏览次数: 2194.0
正文内容:
根据我校发展国际化教育的方针，为培养具有国际视野和国际竞争力的高素质人才，2017年夏季，钱学森学院将选派4名优秀在校生，前往日本KDDI研究所参加暑期项目的交流学习。

日本KDDI研究所简介(KDDI R&D Laboratories Inc.)
In order to respond to the drastic changes of information technology (IT), especially in the mobile world, as its key strategies, KDDI has come up with the 3M strategies to realize Multi-networks, Multi-devices, and Multi-usages (3M), as well as globalization to expand its business deployment around the world. KDDI R&D Laboratories is promoting research and development to create low-cost, highly added value, reliable and high-quality networks and services for the 3M strategies. It will also foresee trends addressing the leading-edge technology development that is required for dreams-come-true services to the next generation. Furthermore, KDDI R&D Laboratories will contribute to its global strategy by actively addressing open innovation overseas, such as in San Francisco in the United States.
In today’s rapidly advancing information technologies, KDDI R&D Laboratories is going ahead with “Challenge for Innovation” so that it will be able to provide safe, secure, and comfortable leading-edge values and experiences.
The business of KDDI R&D Laboratories Inc. involves:
žCommission of R&D projects;
žProduct development and technical support;
žEducation, training, and consulting in the field of telecommunication systems;
žProvision of intellectual property rights, including patents and copyrights;
žDirect sales of products, disclosure, and transfer of technologies resulting from its research.

项目实验室简介(The Intelligent Media Laboratory)
The main mission of the group is tounderstand humans, based on various data that can be collected fromsmartphones and wearable devices, using machine learning technologies.

项目时间：2017年7月24日—8月18日（共四周）
选拔条件：
1.工科试验班（钱学森班）、少年班、数学试验班学生，要求为大二、大三在读。其中，工科试验班（钱学森班）2人，少年班1人，数学试验班1人。
2.专业不限。
费用情况：
无需缴纳学费；钱学森学院承担往返旅费；日本KDDI研究所承担交流期间的住宿费；其他费用自理。
报名截止时间：2017年4月24日
报名方式：
提交以下材料的纸质版和电子版：钱学森学院学生国（境）外交流项目报名表、中英文学业成绩单、中英文个人简历、英语等级考试成绩单。
报名材料请认真准备。钱学森学院选拔结束后，日本KDDI研究所将进行复审，以确保交流人员符合对方项目的要求。
